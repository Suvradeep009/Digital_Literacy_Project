The DOs

* **Secure your accounts:** Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) and review your privacy settings regularly.
* **Think before posting:** Assume everything you post is permanent and could be seen by anyone.
* **Curate your feed:** Unfollow negativity; engage with content that inspires and educates you.
* **Fact-check:** Verify information before hitting share to avoid spreading rumors.
* **Engage respectfully:** Build genuine connections and pause before reacting to inflammatory posts.


The DON'Ts

* **Don't overshare:** Keep your address, phone number, financial details, and sensitive documents offline.
* **Don't post real-time locations:** Wait until you leave a place to post photos for your own safety.
* **Don't feed the trolls:** Ignore, block, and move on from people looking for an argument.
* **Don't compare yourself:** Remember that social media is a highly curated highlight reel, not everyday reality.
* **Don't click suspicious links:** Be cautious of strange messages or links, even if they come from a friend's account.
